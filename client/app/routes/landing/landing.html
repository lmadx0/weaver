Intelligence agencies
In national security, missing a link can mean the difference between stopping a crisis and watching it happen. But with such a high volume of information, it’s hard to see the signal through the noise. By overlaying internal reports and external news sources, analysts can compare information and see who relates to whom.
 


News organizations
Reporters know that a story is a complicated thing. Not every rumor is truth, but any whisper could lead to the next big break. IBM Weaver extracts events from large bodies of text and displays them by time and location, allowing reporters to easily unravel stories, track inconsistencies, and find the truth.
 


Emergency responders
When a crisis does hit, emergency responders face both literal and figurative floods. Information comes in from all sources, and constrained resources and time means a limited ability to manage it. IBM Weaver parses incoming information by location, time, and entity, allowing incident managers to prioritize and direct resources efficiently.
